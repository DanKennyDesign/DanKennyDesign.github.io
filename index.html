You can see some examples of my work below.

Eden



Eden is a 'Mind Painting' produced with the University of Sydney as well as Ramus Lighting for the Digital Wall in Sydney's Central Park. Users can put on a Mindwave device and use their mind to stimulate the virtual garden to grow.

Interactive Storefront



This proposal is designed to put the visual real estate at retail shopfronts to better use. I helped develop a pitch that used new technologies to allow pedestrians to generate original music using their body and take tracks home.
